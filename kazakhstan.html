Kazakhstan Astana Hub

0% corporate tax for tech startups. Digital Nomad Visa and scaling support.

Astana Hub is the largest support center for IT companies and tech businesses in Kazakhstan, operating as an international technopark. The hub provides comprehensive support including 0% corporate tax, access to investments, infrastructure, community, and scaling solutions.

Target Audience: Tech startups, IT companies, entrepreneurs, digital nomads, and foreign specialists looking for tax-free environment with visa support and scaling opportunities.

Key Benefits

  • 0% corporate tax for hub participants
  • 0% individual income tax and social tax for foreign employees
  • Digital Nomad Visa for foreign specialists and families
  • Fully electronic registration (no originals required)
  • Extraterritoriality - operate from any city in Kazakhstan
  • Access to investments and scaling programs

Can You Open & Manage Your Business Fully Online?

‚úì YES - Fully Online Setup & Management

Setting Up Your Company Online

100% electronic process. All document flow is conducted electronically - originals are not required. The entire application can be submitted and processed online.

  • No physical visit required for registration
  • Electronic document submission
  • Application review: up to 15 business days
  • No registration fee

Physical Presence Requirements

None required for registration. However, if you want to relocate to Kazakhstan, Astana Hub offers a Digital Nomad Visa that allows legal residence and work.

Digital Nomad Visa

Optional but valuable: This visa allows foreign specialists to legally live and work in Kazakhstan for the entire period without leaving. Family members (spouses, children) are also entitled to guest visas.

  • Live and work legally in Kazakhstan
  • Bring family members
  • No income tax on employment income
  • No social tax burden for employers

Managing Your Business Remotely

Full remote management. The hub operates on the principle of extraterritoriality, meaning registered companies can be based in any city within Kazakhstan while still enjoying hub benefits.

  • Operate from anywhere in Kazakhstan or abroad
  • Electronic document management
  • Remote access to all hub services
  • No requirement for physical office in Astana

üí° Best of Both Worlds

You can register and manage remotely, or relocate with the Digital Nomad Visa for full legal residence while enjoying 0% taxation.

Program Details

Official Program Name

Astana Hub International Technopark - Special legal regime for IT companies

Legal Framework

Established by the Government of Kazakhstan as an international technopark with special tax regime. Operates under the Law on Supporting Entrepreneurial Activities.

Eligibility Requirements

  • Company must be IT or tech-focused
  • Can be a new startup or existing business
  • Suitable for companies at any stage (idea to scaling)
  • Foreign companies can participate

Application Process

Review Timeline:

  • Preliminary document review: up to 5 days
  • Expert commission decision: up to 10 days
  • Total: up to 15 business days

Restrictions and Limitations

  • Must maintain participant status requirements
  • Business must remain tech/IT-focused
  • Compliance with hub reporting requirements

Tax Structure: Standard vs Astana Hub

‚ö†Ô∏è IMPORTANT: Astana Hub is a SPECIAL PROGRAM

The 0% tax benefits are NOT automatic for all Kazakhstan companies. You can register a company first (with standard tax rates), THEN apply to become an Astana Hub participant to receive tax exemptions.

Standard Kazakhstan Company Tax Rates (Without Astana Hub)

Tax Type Standard Rate (Regular Company)
Corporate Income Tax 20%
VAT 12%
Social Tax 9.5% of payroll
Individual Income Tax 10%

Astana Hub Participant Tax Rates (Special Program)

Tax Type Rate
Corporate Tax (Astana Hub participant) 0%
Individual Income Tax (foreign specialists) 0%
Social Tax (employers of foreign specialists) 0%
Individual Income Tax (Kazakhstan citizens) Standard rates apply
VAT Standard rates (12%)

Tax Advantages for Foreign Specialists

Significant benefit: Foreign specialists employed by Astana Hub participants enjoy preferential tax treatment:

  • Salaries are exempt from individual income tax
  • Employers benefit from social tax exemption
  • Substantially reduces overall tax burden for relocation

Corporate Tax Benefits

0% corporate tax for all Astana Hub participants. This applies to both local and foreign companies registered as participants.

Duration of Benefits

Benefits continue as long as you maintain Astana Hub participant status. No specified end date currently.

Additional Support

  • Access to investment opportunities
  • Export support programs
  • Employee relocation assistance
  • AI integration support
  • Infrastructure and coworking spaces

Complete Journey: From Foreigner to Astana Hub Participant

‚úì ADVANTAGE: Fully Electronic Process

Unlike Uzbekistan, Kazakhstan allows 100% electronic registration - no physical visit required. All documents can be submitted and processed online.

The Two-Stage Process

Stage 1: Register Company

First, register a Kazakhstan legal entity (can be done online, subject to standard 20% corporate tax)

Stage 2: Apply to Astana Hub

Then apply for Astana Hub participant status to get 0% tax benefits (takes up to 15 days)

Optional: Digital Nomad Visa

If you want to relocate to Kazakhstan while managing your business, you can also apply for the Digital Nomad Visa which allows legal residence with 0% income tax for foreign specialists.

Operating Requirements

Minimum Capital Requirements

Minimal - Kazakhstan has low minimum capital requirements for LLCs.

Annual Reporting Obligations

  • Annual report to Astana Hub on activities
  • Standard Kazakhstan tax filings (even at 0%)
  • Financial reporting as required by law
  • Maintain participant status requirements

Accounting Requirements

Must maintain proper accounting according to Kazakhstan law. English-speaking accountants available. Costs are moderate ($100-300/month).

Local Presence Requirements

Flexible: Thanks to extraterritoriality, you can operate from any city in Kazakhstan. No requirement for physical office in Astana specifically.

Employee Requirements

None mandatory - No requirement to hire Kazakhstan employees. Can operate solo or with remote/foreign team.

Banking & Financial Services

Bank Account Opening

Difficulty: Medium

Can be done in person or remotely depending on the bank. Several modern banks in Kazakhstan offer good services.

Available Banking Options

  • Bank CenterCredit - international services

Payment Processing

Local and international payment systems available. Kazakhstan is developing its fintech sector. Integration with international platforms increasingly available.

Currency Considerations

Kazakhstan uses Tenge (KZT). Multi-currency accounts available at major banks. USD and EUR widely used for business.
